OKAY Stationery Shop

Welcome to our stationery shop, your one-stop destination for all things paper and pen! We are passionate about providing high-quality stationery products that inspire creativity and productivity. From elegant notebooks to vibrant pens, from sleek planners to unique paper crafts, we offer an extensive range of items to cater to every need and style. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a creative enthusiast, our shop is designed to be your haven for all things stationery. With a commitment to exceptional customer service and a love for the art of writing, we strive to make every visit to our shop an enjoyable and inspiring experience.

Our History

2005

In August 2005, okay Stationery Store was established in Malacca. At the beginning, our stationery shop was not well known, but once the demand for correction liquid gradually increased, while other stationery shops did not provide this stationery, our shop became the only stationery seller with correction liquid. After that, more and more people came to our store to buy, which led to a sharp increase in business. From then on, our shop began to sell more and more stationery and gained the support of many people.

2012

In 2012, we opened two branches in Kuala Lumpur and Johor.

2019

In 2019, decreasing sales due to the sudden outbreak of the epidemic also had a huge impact. Therefore, the store created a website to facilitate customers to buy stationery online. With the launch of the website, stationery sales gradually increased. Until now, more and more people use our website to buy the stationery they need in order not to go out.

Our Vision and Mission

Mission: To provide customers with the highest quality stationery and office equipment to meet their needs for learning, work and creativity. At the same time, it also provides customers with quality service and shopping experience.


Vision: To be the community's preferred stationery store and a trusted supplier of stationery and office supplies.
